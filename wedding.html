FAQ

  • When and how do I RSVP?

    Répondez, s'il vous plait. Kindly confirm your attendance or regrets through any of the following:


    • - Patrick Gamara: +63.998.237.8228
    • - Website: RSVP Page

    We look forward to receiving your responses on or before 27 October 2018.
  • Oh no! I have a sudden change of plans on your wedding day. What do I do?
    In case of any change in your response, please inform us on or before 7th of November 2018 so that we can adjust and/or reallocate the seats that we have reserved for you.
  • Can I bring a guest to the wedding?
    Only the assigned seats indicated in the invitation you have received from the couple. Any additional guest may not be accommodated due to limited seating capacity of our venue.

    For further concerns in this regard, kindly coordinate with Patrick (please see first question above).
  • What do I wear?
    Semi-Formal:

    Long or tea-length dress (black only) for the ladies

    Long sleeves polo (black or white), slacks (any color) for the gentlemen
  • Are there are any colors you would prefer me not to wear?
    Ladies, please refrain from wearing white or colorful (red, pink, yellow, etc.) dress/gown. We prefer that you only wear BLACK.

    As for the gentlemen, just choose between WHITE or BLACK long sleeves polo with any color of slacks that matches your attire.
  • Is there a parking area that is conveniently located near the Ceremony Venue (Sta. Rita de Cascia)?
    Yes! There is a parking lot outside the church.
  • Where do I sit at the wedding ceremony?
    Assigned seats will be given for the entourage.

    As for the guests, free seats will be allocated. Please fill the seats nearest to the altar before proceeding to other seats.
  • I can only attend the Wedding Ceremony, is that alright?
    Of course! Any time you allot for our wedding is well-appreciated.

    Do let us know when you respond to our RSVP in case you are joining only a part of our special day.
  • Can I go straight to the reception?
    Certainly! But you’ll miss out on our beautiful wedding ceremony.

    Do let us know when you respond to our RSVP in case you are joining only a part of our special day.
  • What can I do after the wedding ceremony and before the reception?
    You can go straight to Matrix Creations Events Venue. We have refreshments, live entertainment, and photo booth waiting for the guests during the Cocktail Hour.
  • Is there a parking area that is conveniently located near the Reception Venue (Matrix Creations Events Venue)?
    There is a limited parking space. We highly recommend carpooling during the event.
  • Can we bring kids on the wedding day?
    Although we adore your little ones, we request that our wedding would be for adults only so guests can enjoy, drink, and dance without worrying about upsetting little ears.
  • Where do I sit at the reception?
    No worries, we've prepared table and seating arrangements. Please proceed to the registration table upon arrival in Matrix Creations Events Venue.

    We will also be posting the table arrangements online a day or two before the wedding, for convenient reference.
  • Do we bring a gift for the newlyweds?
    With all that we have, we’ve been truly blessed. Your presence and prayers are all that we request. But if you desire to give nonetheless, monetary gift is one we suggest.
  • May I take pictures or videos of the wedding?
    We would like our wedding ceremony to be an unplugged wedding. We invite you to be fully present with us during our ceremony.

    Please turn off all cell phones and cameras. You may start taking pictures and videos in our wedding reception.
  • How can I leave a special message for the newlyweds?
    We have prepared a guest book for your lovely messages thru the photo booth. Please approach any of our coordinators for assistance.

    You may also send us a message through the RSVP page.
  • I have food allergies. How do I avoid food that I am allergic to?
    Avoid food that you know you are not supposed to eat. When in doubt, kindly check the menu and/or inquire from the servers.
  • Ok, I got everything. Can you give me a rundown of the timeline/schedule to remember for the wedding?
    The Wedding Ceremony in the church begins at 9:30 AM.

    The Cocktail hour begins at 12:00 NN. Then. the reception starts at 1:00 PM

    We hope that you will stay with us until the end of the program.
  • I have a question not answered here; how do I contact you?
    For all other queries, or if you just want to drop us a message, a word of advice, or warnings against marriage, please email us at kokoandsani2018@gmail.com.
